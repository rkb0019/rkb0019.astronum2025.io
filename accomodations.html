TRAVEL INFORMATION
Coming the Fluno Center is easy. The Fluno Center is located in the heart of Madison, Wisconsin, on the University of Wisconsin-Madison campus. Here are some travel tips to reach the center:

By Air:
The nearest airport is Dane County Regional Airport (MSN), approximately 15 minutes from the Fluno Center. Taxi, ride-share services, and rental cars are available at the airport. The Chicago O'Hare International Airport (ORD) airport is approximately 2.5 to 3 hours from the center by car or bus or by shuttle services. Connecting flights from ORD to MSN are also availabe and take approximately 1 hour.

By Bus:
Madison is serviced by intercity buses like Greyhound and Badger Bus, which arrive at the Madison Bus Terminal, about a 5-minute drive from the Fluno Center. Madison Metro Transit also provides convenient public bus routes.

By Car:
The Fluno Center is easily accessible by car via major highways, including I-90, I-94, and US-151. Parking is available at the center or nearby parking facilities.

Local Transportation:
Madison offers reliable public transit services through Metro Transit, as well as bike rental options for easy navigation around the city.
